About Me

Hi! I'm a recent high school graduate, excited to begin my next chapter at the University of Texas at Dallas, pursuing a degree in Computer Science. My journey in web development began with simple curiosity—exploring websites and inspecting pages to understand how things worked. What started as fascination quickly became a passion. As I learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I realized the power of bringing ideas to life through code.

Over time, my interests expanded beyond web development. I became captivated by Artificial Intelligence—the idea that machines can think, learn, and even create amazed me. The more I explored, the more I wanted to understand how AI works and what I could build with it.

Now, I'm constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer. Whether it's building user-friendly websites or experimenting with AI tools, I love turning creative ideas into real, functional projects. This is just the beginning, and I'm excited to see where this journey takes me.
